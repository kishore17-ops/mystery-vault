Privacy Policy

This page explains what information Mystery Vault collects, how it's used, and the choices you have.

Mystery Vault ("we," "us," or "the Vault") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect when you visit mystery-vault-one.vercel.app (the "Site"), how we use it, and the choices available to you. By using the Site, you agree to the practices described below.

1. Information We Collect

We collect as little information as possible, and only what is needed to operate the Site and respond to you.

Information You Provide Directly

  • Newsletter signup: If you subscribe, we collect the email address you submit.
  • Contact form: If you send us a message, we collect your name, email address, subject, and message content. Submissions are processed through a third-party form service (Formspree) in order to deliver the message to us.

Information Collected Automatically

Like most websites, our hosting and infrastructure providers may automatically log basic technical information, such as your approximate location, browser type, device type, pages visited, and timestamps, for security and performance purposes. We do not currently use third-party advertising or tracking cookies.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To send the newsletter or case-file updates you signed up for.
  • To respond to messages or tips submitted through the contact form.
  • To maintain the security, performance, and basic functioning of the Site.
  • To understand, in aggregate, how visitors use the Site, so we can improve it.

We do not sell your personal information, and we do not share it with third parties for their own marketing purposes.

3. Third-Party Services

The Site relies on a small number of third-party services to function:

  • Formspree — processes and delivers contact form submissions.
  • Google Fonts — serves the typefaces used on the Site.
  • Hosting provider (Vercel) — hosts the Site and may log standard server access data.

Each of these providers has its own privacy practices, and we encourage you to review them if you have concerns about how they handle data.

4. Cookies

The Site does not currently set its own tracking cookies. Some third-party services listed above may use cookies or similar technologies as part of their normal operation (for example, to deliver fonts or process form submissions). If this changes, we will update this policy.

5. Data Retention

We retain newsletter email addresses for as long as you remain subscribed, and contact form submissions for as long as reasonably necessary to address your inquiry. You can request deletion of your information at any time using the contact details below.

6. Your Choices

  • You can unsubscribe from the newsletter at any time using the unsubscribe link in any email, or by contacting us.
  • You can request access to, correction of, or deletion of the personal information we hold about you by reaching out through the Contact section.

7. Children's Privacy

The Site is intended for a general audience and is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can remove it.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for legal reasons. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page will reflect the most recent revision. Continued use of the Site after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

9. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or how your information is handled, please reach out via the Contact form on the homepage.
